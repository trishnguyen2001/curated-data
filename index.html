THE LOVE I WAS TAUGHT

Love can take many forms.

The love you are taught will become the love you seek.

Most of us first experience love through our families.
They teach us how to perceive love in all its forms -- toxic or not.

Click to relearn how to love.

Love can be kind and cruel -- be careful which edge of the blade you use with those around you.
